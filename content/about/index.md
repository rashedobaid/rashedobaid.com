I manage Cloud and DevOps, with a focus on building reliable, secure, and scalable infrastructure.

I’m a **HashiCorp Terraform Certified Associate** and an **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner**. Most of my work revolves around Infrastructure as Code, cloud architecture, automation, and improving delivery workflows across environments.

I’m especially interested in:

- Terraform module design and maintainability
- AWS and Cloudflare infrastructure
- CI/CD pipelines and release reliability
- Observability and operational excellence
- Cost-aware infrastructure decisions

On this site, I share practical notes from real projects — including Terraform, Cloudflare, networking, and homelab experiments.

If you work in cloud/platform engineering and want to exchange ideas, feel free to connect.
